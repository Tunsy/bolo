Clow
AWARDED BEST BIG DATA HACK AT TREEHACKS 2016
Clow is a clinic-facing app that uses emotion analysis and machine learning to predict patients’ illnesses. When patients sign in before their session, the device will capture an image of the patient’s face and predict the patient’s illness. Its accuracy is trained and improved by matching the actual diagnosis result and the patient’s emotion readings.
